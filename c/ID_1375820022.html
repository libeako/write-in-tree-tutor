Demonstration and Tutorial of WriteInTreeBasic workflowYou write your book's contents in text tree format first. A text tree is a tree of simple text lines.Text Tree Editors

Applications dedicated for simple text tree editing do not current exist [according to my knowledge], but 2 "mindmap editors" do exist.

They are:

Freeplane

Freemind

WriteInTree currently builds and depends on them. You need to use one of them.

They are both more than just text tree editors, they are mindmap editors. Still they can be used simply as text tree editors. They both are free software, they are mature, though they do contain bugs. They run on the Java platform.
