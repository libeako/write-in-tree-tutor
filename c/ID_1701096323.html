Demonstration and Tutorial of WriteInTreeBasic workflow

You write your book's contents in text tree format first. A text tree is a tree of simple text lines.

Text Tree Editors

When the structure and content of your book is ready then you have 2 tasks left:

Review your work in its entirity to improve its quality.

Specify format how you want your book look like when rendered into html website. You do this by attaching special nodes to your mindmap. What special nodes and how they will control the translation into website is defined by the write-in-tree technology.

Often it is best to do these 2 taks at the same time.
